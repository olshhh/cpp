Potential exception sources:

* calls to person.name(), person.grade(), person.salary(), and person.id() may throw exceptions, since Person is a user-defined class
* the function save(...) may throw an exception during execution
* output operations with std::cout and operator<< may throw an exception, for example std::ios_base::failure
* the comparison operators == and > may also throw exceptions if grade() and salary() return user-defined types with overloaded comparison operators
